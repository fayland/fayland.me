--- category: perl layout: post tags: - lame title: downsample for mp3s --- {% include JB/setup %} mm, we use lame to resample/downsample mp3 under linux. (like convert 256 kbps to 128 kbps )
first use MP3::Info to get $bitrate = $info->{BITRATE}.
then use $s = "lame -b 128 $orig_file $tmp_file && mv -f $tmp_file $orig_file && chmod 0777 $orig_file"; for $bitrate > 128
then system($s);

pretty simple but useful.
